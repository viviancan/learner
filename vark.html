VARK: A guide to learning styles

To help determine your preferred learning style, we will be using the VARK Questionnaire. There are no right or wrong answers. We are simply using the results as a baseline. To learn more about the VARK questionnaire, click here.


(Prophetic technology, 2017)

The VARK results are broken into the following modalities:

  • Visual (V)
  • Aural / Auditory (A)
  • Read / Write (R)
  • Kinesthetic (K)
