VARK: A guide to learning styles

To help determine your preferred learning style, we will be using the VARK Questionnaire. The delivery of content for this course will be tailored based on your results. There are no right or wrong answers. We are simply using the results as a baseline. To learn more about the VARK questionnaire, click here.

The VARK results are broken into the following modalities:

  • Visual (V)
  • Aural / Auditory (A)
  • Read / Write (R)
  • Kinesthetic (K)
