What is User Centered Design (UCD)?

User Centered Design (UCD) is a design framework, which uses a holistic approach to design a positive and intuitive experience that is unique to the specific audience of your product.

UCD Principles
(Novoseltseva, 2017)

  1. Early focus on users and tasks
  2. Incorporating user feedback
  3. Allowing users to take an active role early in the development
  4. Iterative design

How does it relate to LCD?

Surprisingly, many of the concepts relate to learner-centered design (LCD). Throughout the course, we will relate these concepts back to the LCD framework. Moving forward, you will need a something to write on and something to write with.
