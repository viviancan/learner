UCD Design Process

Stage 4: Evaluation & Feedback

Evaluation

During evaluation you will evaluate the effectiveness of your site. This can be accomplished through user testing and user feedback. Additionally, you can evaluate based on different critera. For example:

  • Is your design intuitive?
  • Can the a visitor to your site accomplish what they came there to do?
  • Can these tasks be accomplished quickly?
  • Do your users like your site?
After you have answered these questions, you can return to the design phase to update your current content and flow.
