Foundations of UCD

(1) Early focus on users and tasks

Plan. Plan. Plan.

The first step in the UCD process is to plan. Since you will be designing a site within the UCD framework, it is important to answer some questions, while always keeping the user in mind.

As mentioned before, the follow along activity for this course will help you get started designing a site for whatever you want. Maybe you need an updated classroom site, or are interesting in blogging, or want somewhere to showcase your work! The choice is yours. By the end of this course, you will have the beginnings of a great site built within the UCD framework.

Activity 1A
Please answer the questions before continuing.

  1. What is the purpose of your site?
  2. What is the vision for your site?
  3. What are the goals for your site?
  4. What is the overall feeling of your site?
